Pillars of Newsify!

Newsify innovative news search features allow you to spend less time finding the news and more time reading it. You're only ever one tap away from finding the most important current events of whatever genre you choose, or no genre if you browse our homepage.

Newsify pulls articles from sites of all political leanings to provide readers with an unbiased viewpoint. This is a key part of our goal to help readers from both sides of the aisle to better understand each other. We don't personalize articles based off user preferences because we believe in forcing our readers to leave their bubbles and get news from more than one source.

The only data newsify tracks about you is what you give it. We believe in the importance of privacy and that is why the only information we track about you is your stock preferences which you can choose to input. Cookies are used, but are exclusively for the purpose of keeping you logged in.
